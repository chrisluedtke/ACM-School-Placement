Step 2 of 3: ACM Survey Data

Before uploading your survey data, ensure your survey results are clean. Check that no ACM took the survey twice, and if two ACMs have the same name, add a digit to make each name unique (i.e. John Smith1, John Smith2). If you did not use the SurveyGizmo survey provided by HQ, you must ensure the headers and values of your survey results exactly match the expected values. Review the information in this guide, and make any necessary changes to your survey data.

Upload your survey data file (.csv) and continue.
