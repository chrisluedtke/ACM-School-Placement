Step 2 of 3: ACM Survey Data

Before uploading your survey data, ensure your survey results are clean:

  • Check that no ACM took the survey twice, and if two ACMs have the same name, change them to make each name unique (i.e. John Smith1, John Smith2).
  • If you did not use the SurveyGizmo survey provided by HQ, you must ensure the column names and values match the expected values in this spreadsheet.
  • To ensure certain ACMs get placed at specific schools, add a column called "Manual.Placement" to your survey results, and type the name of the school as it appears in the School Data spreadsheet from Step 1.

Upload your survey data file (.csv) and continue.
